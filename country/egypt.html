Best Months to Visit Egypt

January

Mild weather, perfect for sightseeing without crowds.

February

Ideal for visiting pyramids and cruising the Nile.

March

Spring warmth with blossoming landscapes.

April

Great beach weather and cultural festivals.

May

Sunshine and fewer tourists for desert adventures.

June

Hot weather perfect for Red Sea diving.

July

Beach resorts with summer deals.

August

Warmest month; relax by sea or pool.

September

Ideal weather for exploring ancient sites.

October

Balanced weather and golden scenery.

November

Great visibility for diving, mild inland temps.

December

Festive season with cultural highlights.
