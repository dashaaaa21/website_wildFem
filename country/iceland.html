Iceland

Land of fire and ice — explore glaciers, volcanoes, and northern lights.

Natural Wonders of Iceland

Blue Lagoon

Soak in the warm, mineral-rich waters surrounded by lava fields and steam clouds.

Gullfoss Waterfall

Marvel at the golden cascade plunging into a dramatic canyon — a top Golden Circle stop.

Vatnajökull Glacier

Europe's largest glacier offers stunning ice caves and hiking adventures.

Taste of Iceland

Icelandic Lamb

Free-range and grass-fed, lamb is the heart of traditional Icelandic cuisine.

Skyr Yogurt

Thick, creamy, and rich in protein — enjoy it sweet or savory.

Travel Tips for Iceland

  • Dress in Layers: Weather can change rapidly, so be prepared for sun, rain, and snow — all in one day.
  • Rent a 4x4: Some scenic routes require an off-road capable vehicle, especially in winter.
  • Respect Nature: Stick to paths and avoid damaging the fragile moss.
  • Stay Safe: Always check road and weather conditions before heading out.
